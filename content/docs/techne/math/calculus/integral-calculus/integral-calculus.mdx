## 反常积分
{/* 
$$$
\int_1^{+\infty}\dfrac{dx}{x^{p}}\implies\left\{\begin{array}{l}p>1\implies A p\le1\implies D\end{array}\right.\qquad\int_0^1\dfrac{dx}{x^{p}}\implies\left\{\begin{array}{l}p\ge1\implies A 0<p<1\implies D \end{array}\right.
$$$

$$$
\int_2^{+\infty}\dfrac{dx}{x\ln^{p}x}\implies\left\{\begin{array}{l}p>1\implies A p\le1\implies D \end{array}\right.
$$$ */}

> $A$表示收敛， $D$表示发散。相关：级数
